# Master Services Agreement (MSA) — outline (draft)

**Between:** Grand Gaia LLC and Customer entity  


---

## Common article headings

1. Definitions  
2. Services; access; service levels (optional reference to SLA exhibit)  
3. Customer obligations; acceptable use  
4. Fees; payment; expenses; audits (if any)  
5. Term; termination; survival  
6. Confidential Information  
7. Intellectual property rights  
8. Data protection; security; privacy (reference DPA / Privacy Policy)  
9. Warranties; disclaimer  
10. Limitation of liability  
11. Indemnification  
12. Insurance (optional)  
13. General: notices, assignment, force majeure, amendments, governing law, jurisdiction, export, anti-bribery, entire agreement, **order of precedence** (Order Form vs MSA)  

---

## Order of precedence (example clause topic)

Counsel often specifies: **conflicts** between Order Form, MSA, and online terms are resolved in a stated order (e.g., Order Form controls for commercial terms; MSA for general legal framework).
